About

I am a research scientist with 10 years of experience working in the field of statistical physics. I am passionate about tackling fundamental problems in an interdisciplinary setting. My research has bridged the fields of physics, chemistry, materials science, and engineering, where I've worked on problems ranging from crystallization to energy storage. I also have a deep interest in scientific computing, data visualization, and open source software development.

Most recently I worked as a Research Officer at the University of Bath. Before that, I was a Project Scientist at the Molecular Foundry, Lawrence Berkeley National Laboratory, and a Postdoctoral Scholar at the UC Berkeley.
